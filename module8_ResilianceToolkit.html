Deep Breathing

.🌬️🫁Breathe in calm, breathe out stress.Take a moment. Inhale slowly through your nose, letting your belly rise. Hold for a few seconds. Then exhale gently through your mouth, feeling your body soften..🌬️🫁

Try the 4-7-8 technique:

  • 🫁 Inhale for 4 seconds
  • ⏸️ Hold for 7 seconds
  • 🌫️ Exhale for 8 seconds

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Name 5 things you can see... 👁️

Name 4 things you can touch... 👐

Name 3 things you can hear... 👂

Name 2 things you can smell... 👃

Name 1 things you can taste... 👅

The 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique is a simple mindfulness tool that uses your five senses to pull your attention away from anxious thoughts and back to the present moment.🧠🧭
