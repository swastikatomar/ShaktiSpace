FAQ

What is a normal menstrual cycle?

A normal menstrual cycle ranges from 21 to 35 days, with bleeding lasting 3 to 7 days.

How can I relieve menstrual cramps?

Applying heat, drinking herbal teas, or taking over-the-counter pain medications can help relieve cramps.
